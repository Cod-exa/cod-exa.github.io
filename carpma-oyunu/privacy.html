Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Erdinç Topal ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the "Çarpma Oyunu" (Multiplication Game) mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: We do not actively collect personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or physical address.

Usage Data & Analytics: The App may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., playtime, scores, error reports) to help us improve the educational experience. This data is collected locally first and may be sent to analytics services asynchronously.

2. Third-Party Services (Ads)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data (such as advertising IDs and IP addresses) to provide personalized ads. When you first use the App (if located in the EU/EEA), we request your consent in accordance with GDPR via the Google UMP SDK.

  • You can view Google's advertising privacy policy here: Google Ads Policy
  • You can change your ad consent choices at any time from the 'Settings' menu within the App.

3. Children's Privacy

While this App is designed as an educational tool for learning multiplication, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Given the presence of ads, we adhere to COPPA guidelines regarding the serving of ads to minors where applicable.

4. Data Storage

Your game progress (scores, unlocked badges, combos) is saved locally on your device to ensure you can track your learning journey. This data is not uploaded to our servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support mechanisms provided in the applicable App Store, or via Erdinç Topal's developer page.
